Eren Information

Eren Jaeger is a boy that was born in Paradis, an island isolated from the walls He lived in SHiganshina district with his parents Grisha and Carla Jaeger, with his childhood friends Armin Arlert and Mikasa Ackerman. This all changed after the fall of Wall Maria which caused Titans to enter into the district and eat many people including his mum. His father Grisha then gave hima key to the basement in their home and injected him with some titan Fluid and after Eren became a titan he ate his dad and got the abilities of the Founding and Attack titan although he couldn't use the founding Titan's powers

Fast forward many years later, Eren, Mikasa and Armin (best boy) joined the survey corps Many years later, Eren decided to sacrifice himself and to use his Titan abilities to start the rumbling and destroy the world in order to save Armin and Mikasa as well as all the Eldians and the citizens of paradis. He is officially known as the Savior of Eldia. TATAKAE.
